Renowned for its murals of the Kandyan Sittara art, “Gangarama Raja Maha Viharaya” is attributed to King Kirti Sri Rajasinghe (1747–1781).
The temple is named “Gangarama” due to its location near the great Mahaweli River in Lewella.

About Us

Gangarama Raja Maha Viharaya is a historic temple renowned for its stunning murals and rich cultural heritage. Located near the Mahaweli River in Lewella, the temple has been a significant spiritual and cultural center for centuries.

Our temple is a testament to the artistic and architectural brilliance of the Kandyan era, with intricate designs and paintings that captivate visitors from around the world.
